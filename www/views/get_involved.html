Welcome to our community!

We are a vibrant and growing group of volunteers, motivated by the desire of having better tools to create web sites and apps. If you have ever thought that the current web standards are just not good enough, you can help us make a change!

1.Join us on IRC

Our IRC channel is the place where most of the discussions take place. There's almost always someone online so don't hesitate to drop by.

#AXR @ irc.freenode.net

You can either use your own IRC client or the online webchat. The IRC logs are available at files.axrproject.org/irc.

2.Fork us on GitHub

axr/core

The library that drives the essential functions for AXR.

axr/browser

A basic browser used to view AXR pages and provide developers a means to test the AXR library.

axr/specification

This is where we work on the HSS specification.
